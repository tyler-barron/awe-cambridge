03 — Gather

Awe Talks

Small gatherings to share what moves you — no agenda, no slides. Someone shares a moment of awe; others listen.

🕯
What is an Awe Talk?
Usually 4–12 people in a café, home, or park. Someone opens with a prompt — a moment that stopped them. Others share in turn. Inspired by Dacher Keltner's research on how awe expands our sense of shared humanity.
