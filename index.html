Awe in Cambridge

The Awe Atlas

Cambridge is full of places that stop people in their tracks. This is a set of tools for finding them, mapping them, and understanding what it is about those places that moves us.

Tool 01
Awe Atlas
Find and create everyday awe. Browse instances near you, join or schedule awe walks, attend or host awe talks, and contribute your own moments of wonder to the atlas.

Tool 02
Interactive Map
Explore 220 georeferenced instances of awe plotted across Cambridge. Filter by all analytical dimensions — geography, morphology, phenomenology, and temporality.

Tool 03
Alluvial Explorer
Visualize how instances of awe flow across analytical categories — wonder type, physical form, characteristics, temporality, and more. Toggle and reorder dimensions to reveal patterns in the data.
